Sustainable Luxury

The interior design wing of Woven Design Collaborative looks at design as a holistic experience that brings spaces alive with character. We work collaboratively with our clients and craftsmen to weave elements that reflect their personality and style. We believe that space is the physical extension of the soul and the two share an intricate relationship. Our founder Nisha Mary Poulose brings the nuances of her culture and identity into the design process. She upholds values of sustainability and ecological harmony through her designs, and her spaces reflect and infuse natural elements in stratified ways. Nisha’s tropical influences from her adolescent years in Kerala and her global experiences as an urban planner play with each other to create tailored spatial experiences. WDC interiors is bold yet sensitive and our designs create a palate that permeates the boundaries of practicality to create finesse and luxury.

NISHA MARY POULOSE

Nisha is an Architect and an Erasmus Mundus Scholar with a double masters in Regional Planning and Urban Development from Germany and France. She has over 10 years of local and international experience in the field, working with boutique designer firms as well as independently. She looks at design as a process that creates holistic experiences where design meets need. She enjoys working with clients and bridging their requirements with elements of their personalities to create bespoke spaces.
